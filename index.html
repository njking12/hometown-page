St. John's

City Information

  • It is the Capital city of Newfoundland and Labrador.
  • The current mayor is Danny Breen.
  • The population is approximately 212,579.
  • It is the most easterly city in North America.

St. John's is one of the oldest cities in North America. Known for it's natural beauty and rich heritage, it has a charm unlike anywhere else.
